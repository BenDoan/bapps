Setup Instructions

  1. Step 1: Install the Gauntlet Performance Tracker plugin in RuneLite
  2. Step 2: Open the plugin settings and enable "Save results to file"
  3. Step 3: Select files from your RuneLite folder:

    Windows:
    %UserProfile%\.runelite\gauntletPerformanceTracker

    macOS:
    $HOME/.runelite/gauntletPerformanceTracker
